05 March 2022

Alliostra updated:

  • Adds new plastic goggles
  • 4 varieties of tropical shirts to the drip vendor
  • 3 varieties of puffy coats to the loadout.
  • 3 varieties of sneakers to the loadout.
  • Adds rapid 3-round-bursts to the Sol

Humonitarian updated:

  • hunting rifle and PD rifle crates now contain Tosshin and Vipr respectively
  • changed the prices of the hunting and PD rifle crates, increased price of tosshin, reduced price of Vipr
  • OR guns are no longer FS
  • fixed some typos
  • emitter no longer hits low walls
  • Two new magazine types: extended .20 magazines for the vintorez and wintermute, as well as .30 drum magazines exclusively for the Krinkov
  • Severity 4 explosions implemented
  • blast and explosive grenades now less deadly

Hyperio updated:

  • New sprite for deep drill by CeUvi
  • Mining turret that shoots golems on sight.
  • Removed old mining drill from the game
  • Fix issue with plasma golem not exploding properly without atmosphere
  • Reduce drill digging radius from 7 to 4
  • Add OneStar stalkers
  • Asteroid and exoplanet turfs are now indestructible
  • Fix issue with JTB portals when releasing too soon

Trilbyspaceclone updated:

  • Mercenaries now have a radio with their own privet channel rather then needing to buy it form their uplinks - But its more limited then the version they do buy

hyperioo updated:

  • Covers now trigger properly

04 March 2022

  • fixed .60 ammo boxes not containing as many shells as they should
  • moved the embedded smg to gun crafting window, made it easier to craft

ValoTheValo updated:

  • Fixed NVGs costing biomatter to make in the protolathe

26 February 2022

Alliostra updated:

  • doubled the maximum amount of walls bullets are able to pierce and piercing a wall no longer makes the bullet lose AP.
  • made AMR ammo from the uplink cheaper and increased the damage of the Breacher shells
  • tripled the penetration power of the AMR
  • using a scope makes shots more accurate now
  • the talking spider and the control spiders will no longer be triggered when activating all carrion spiders.
  • Some QOL changes to the carrion spider activation menu.
  • fixed a bug with zooming out
  • buffed the blight spider
  • lowered the prices of some carrion spiders

Humonitarian updated:

  • recoil recovery and recoil from running adjusted
  • removed extra penetration from vigilance
  • vigilance increases accuracy
  • alcohol gives more sanity
  • alcohol more lethal
  • lore-accurate gargleblaster
  • serb vendor vodka price increased to match increased sanity gain
  • NT "Hellfire" grenade
  • NT "Holy Light" grenades are weaker against the Faithful
  • More NT flavour descriptions
  • cover triggers properly

Hyperio updated:

  • Double time interval between burrows for all waves
  • Double time interval between golem spawn for all waves
  • Double drill health
  • Increased mineral multiplier
  • Golems no longer try to destry golem burrows in their way
  • Drill has three health stage. If its healths falls under 66%, you can only repair it up to 66%. If it falls under 33%, you can only repair it up to 33%.
  • Fixed microwave and fire barrel secure unsecure verbs
  • Fixed hydroponics tray secure unsecure verbs
  • Added warning when trying to spawn without brain or eyes
  • Fixed ricochet text
  • Remove cables and pipes from planetary ruins to reduce server load.
  • Replace boombas by other roombas to avoid undesired roundstart explosions

Sparrowbird611 updated:

  • Rebalance armor numbers. In general, lower tier armors should be more valuable now, and some things that didn't seem like they should be so armored were reduced.
  • Gave lasers to compensate for armor change, plasma already has enough to be sustainable.
  • Removed ablative's ability to deflect projectiles, to make it less of a hard counter to certain weapons.
  • Renamed heavy armor vest to mercenary armor vest.
